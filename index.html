Our Story

A neighbourhood gem on the corner of Footscray

The Local Cafe is exactly what it says on the tin — a place that feels like home. We're not here to reinvent anything; we're just making really good bánh mì, pouring honest coffee, and serving the people who walk through our door.

Our bánh mì are the star — crispy baguettes stuffed with your choice of roasted pork, BBQ chicken, tofu, and more, all loaded with fresh herbs, pickled veggies, and our house sauces. Pair it with a flat white and you're set.

Whether you're a Footscray local or just passing through, pull up a chair. We've got a resident pup who might greet you at the door too.
